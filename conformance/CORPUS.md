# Extended Activity-Coverage Corpus

A broad library of real-world activities, each asserted to **round-trip losslessly**
through canonical OpenBody (SPEC §8.3). This is the **executable form of the "never
inferior to prior art" gate** (§3.1) — **coverage validation, not a conformance bar**.

Seeded from the project's activity-coverage corpus (the stress-test material):
e.g. Highland games, kabaddi, BFR training, freediving, isokinetic work, AMRAP/EMOM
metcons, climbing send/attempt/flash, swimming intervals, adaptive/para-sport
substitutions, velocity-based training, greasing-the-groove, multi-participant /
collaborative work.

## Status

**Stub — filled out once a reference implementation exists.** Each entry will be a
record (or record set) plus the assertion that it canonicalizes and round-trips with
no loss. Coverage gaps found here feed back into the standard's change process, not a
pass/fail conformance claim.
